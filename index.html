Hello. I’m Neol.

Seventeen.

Not in college yet. Just trying to move different while most people are still asleep to the bigger picture.

I’m working on my body

Not for looks, but because it sharpens me. Keeps my mind from drifting. Makes the mirror feel honest.

My days are mostly quiet. Some people think that’s a mystery thing. It’s not. I just don’t like saying things that don’t matter.

I’m building something. Not rushing it, but it’s there. In the background, moving. One decision at a time.

She’s in the picture too. Not loud, not public, but steady. She gets the version of me no one else really earns.

Some things I stand by:

  • Stop explaining yourself.
  • The right people already get it.
  • Don’t chase aesthetics. Become undeniable through consistency.
  • You don’t need more clothes. Or more opinions. Or more noise.
  • Clarity comes in silence.

This year’s about refining. Cutting the excess. Thinking sharper. Moving smarter.
