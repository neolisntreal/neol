hello. i’m neol.
seventeen.
not in college yet.
just trying to move different while most people are still asleep to the bigger picture.

i’m working on my body
not for looks
but because it sharpens me
keeps my mind from drifting
makes the mirror feel honest

my days are mostly quiet
some people think that’s a mystery thing
it’s not
i just don’t like saying things that don’t matter

i’m building something
not rushing it
but it’s there
in the background, moving
one decision at a time

she’s in the picture too
not loud, not public
but steady
she gets the version of me no one else really earns

some things i stand by:
– stop explaining yourself
– the right people already get it
– don’t chase aesthetics. become undeniable through consistency
– you don’t need more clothes. or more opinions. or more noise
– clarity comes in silence

this year’s about refining
cutting the excess
thinking sharper
moving smarter
